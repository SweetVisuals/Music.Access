# Goal, Roadmap & Status

## The Vision
**To become the global infrastructure for the independent music economy.**
We aim to democratize the business side of music, giving bedroom producers and independent artists the same tools, insights, and legal protections as major label signees, all powered by an intelligent AI backbone.

---

## Where We Are Now
**Current Status: Pre-Launch Polish & Scaling**

*   **Mobile-First Architecture:** Successfully refactored the legacy desktop application into a fully responsive Mobile interaction model (V4.0 Mobile Support).
*   **Core Feature Parity:** Completed Marketplace, Studio, Contracts, and Collaboration modules.
*   **Infrastructure:** Robust database and AI integration established.

## The Execution Plan (FY 2026)

### Q1: Development Finalization
*   **Focus:** Finish development of both the mobile and desktop applications.
*   **Key Deliverables:** 
    *   Complete feature parity across devices.
    *   Polish UI/UX for "Avant-Garde" aesthetic.
    *   Finalize all Row Level Security (RLS) and backend permissions.

### Q2: Testing & Early Adopters
*   **Focus:** Rigorous testing and onboarding early adopters.
*   **Key Deliverables:** 
    *   **Beta Testing Program:** Onboard 500-1000 selected beta users (Artists & Producers).
    *   **Stress Testing:** Validate high-concurrency performance for real-time features.
    *   **Feedback Loop:** Iterate on features based on direct user feedback from the "inner circle" of early adopters.

### Q3: Public Launch
*   **Focus:** Launch and market app on App Store & go live on public domain.
*   **Key Deliverables:** 
    *   **App Store Deployment:** Global availability on iOS and Android.
    *   **Grand Opening:** Public web launch.
    *   **Marketing Push:** Influencer campaigns and targeted ads to drive initial user acquisition.

### Q4: Growth & Scale
*   **Focus:** Market expansion, Funding & Strategic Growth.
*   **Key Deliverables:** 
    *   **Series A/Seed Funding:** Leverage user metrics from Q3 to secure institutional capital.
    *   **Aggressive Marketing:** Scale user base through paid acquisition and viral loops ("Collaboration" features).
    *   **Partnerships:** Establish key partnerships with distributors or hardware manufacturers.

## 5-Year Horizon (2027 - 2030)

### 2027: The Service Economy & Expansion
Transitioning from a tool for "creation" to a hub for "employment." We will launch the comprehensive **Service Exchange**, allowing engineers, vocalists, and session musicians to sell their time.
*   **Goal:** 60,000 Active Users.
*   **Key Feature:** Gig marketplace with escrow payments.

### 2028: Ecosystem Integration
Breaking the walls between the Studio and the World. Deep integrations with hardware (MIDI controllers mapping directly to our Web DAW) and DSPs.
*   **Goal:** 150,000 Active Users.
*   **Key Feature:** "One-Click Distribution" to Spotify/Apple directly from the project file.

### 2029: FinTech & Financial Sovereignty 
Becoming the bank for the creative class. Offering financial products that traditional banks deny to artists.
*   **Goal:** 300,000 Active Users.
*   **Key Feature:** "Artist Advance" loans based on projected royalties and simplified tax/split management for collectives.
